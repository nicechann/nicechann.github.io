MellowFilm Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

Overview

MellowFilm is a film camera and photo/video editor app. The app is designed to process photos and videos on your device.

Data Collection

MellowFilm does not require account registration and does not collect personal information through its own server.

Photos and Videos

Photos and videos selected or captured in the app are used only for editing, filtering, saving, and sharing features on your device.

Permissions

MellowFilm may request access to the camera and photo library to provide capture, editing, and saving features.

In-App Purchases

MellowFilm may offer optional in-app purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Apple through the App Store.
