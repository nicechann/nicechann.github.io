MellowDay Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

MellowDay is designed to help you record, import, edit, save, and share daily vlog videos. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information related to those features.

Photos, Videos, Camera, and Microphone

MellowDay requests access to your camera, microphone, and photo library only to provide app features such as recording clips, importing existing photos and videos, creating daily vlog videos, and saving completed videos to your photo library.

Local Processing

Photos, videos, and clips are processed on your device for editing, exporting, saving, and sharing. MellowDay does not require an account login.

In-App Purchases

MellowDay Pro purchases and purchase restoration are handled through Apple’s App Store in-app purchase system. Payment information is processed by Apple, not by MellowDay.

Data Sharing

MellowDay does not sell your personal information. When you use the system share sheet or share to another app, the selected content is handled according to the destination app or service you choose.

Children’s Privacy

MellowDay is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or need support, please contact us by email.
