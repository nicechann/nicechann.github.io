▣MellowDay

Turn moments into a daily vlog.

MellowDay is a daily vlog camera app that turns short clips, photos, and videos into one simple video story.

About MellowDay

Record quick clips, import photos and videos, organize today’s moments, apply film-tone filters, set a cover, and create a daily vlog without a complicated editing process.

Key Features

  • Record short daily clips
  • Import photos and videos from your library
  • Capture 1s, 3s, 5s, and 10s clips
  • Use a 4:3 framing guide, timer, filters, and manual controls
  • Apply film-tone filters and adjust effects
  • Save today’s title, set a cover clip, and reorder clips
  • Revisit daily records by date
  • Preview, save, and share today’s completed video
