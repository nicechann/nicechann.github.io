MellowFilm

Film camera filters and photo/video editor

MellowFilm is a film-inspired camera and editor for creating soft, nostalgic photos and videos. Browse curated filters, preview looks in real time, capture photos and videos, edit existing media, and fine-tune your own film tone.

About MellowFilm

MellowFilm is designed for natural color, soft texture, and everyday film mood. It helps you add warm tones, subtle grain, analog-style colors, date stamps, borders, and film-inspired effects to your photos and videos.

Key Features

  • Film-inspired filters for selfies, travel, cafés, nature, night scenes, and everyday moments
  • Real-time camera preview for checking the look before taking a photo or video
  • Photo and video editing with imported media from your library
  • Filter intensity, LUT strength, blur, glow, grain, exposure, brightness, contrast, and white balance controls
  • Date stamps, white borders, and film frames for a finished analog look
  • Curated filter collections organized by mood and scene
  • Recipe saving for frequently used filter and adjustment settings
